Who I Am

About Me

I am an ACCA Member and Oracle Fusion Cloud Consultant with over 7 years of experience in ERP implementation, finance transformation, and accounting solutions. My career began at Capital Accounting & Business Consulting Services and was shaped at A.F. Ferguson & Co. (PwC Pakistan), where I delivered four complete end-to-end Oracle Fusion implementations.

I have since led Oracle ERP engagements at TATA Pakistan and Gerry's Group, managing teams of up to 8 consultants. Today I work as an independent consultant serving clients in the UAE, USA, Saudi Arabia, Canada, and Australia.

Beyond enterprise ERP, I bring hands-on expertise in Microsoft Excel, Xero, QuickBooks, financial modelling, and bookkeeping — effective for both SMEs and large enterprises.
